Designer Research Essay


Your aim will be to write a 750 word essay about a designer from the list below and then 'design' your essay in the style of the designer that you have chosen to research.

Your essay should fit in a 'booklet' of one 11 x 17 sheet. Use folding, color, typography and images to enlighten your audience about this designer’s work. You will print + fold enough copies for the class to read the day that you present. Design in Indesign and make sure you spell check before you print.
