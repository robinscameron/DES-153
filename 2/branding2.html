World Building Brand Project



In order to come up with a brand idea for a start-up that is either a product or service for your final project. You'll meet speed-dating style with three other students and do some focus group style research. In these quick meetings you will ask the following five questions:

  • 1. What is something you wish you had?
  • 2. What is something that you noticed in your life that needs improvement?
  • 3. If it was a service what went particularly well?
  • 4. If it was a product what do you like about it?
  • 5. If you were in charge and could make a product or service — What would you do?


From this quick research you will build a brand and it's world to make your brand come to life. World building helps to make the product or service seem real. We will look at some examples in class.

Firstly, work through researching the realm of your chosen product or service to see what the competition would be. Keep your research images & notes in your sketchbook. Then, create a written document for your brand :
  • 1. Brand Strategy (Answer Who? = Demographics & Why?)
  • 2. Brand Values (3 key words succinctly describe it)
  • 3. Brand Idea (World Building How? Tone, Art Direction)


You will have an opportunity to take some photographs with a photographer for your project in a mini-photoshoot for your brand. You can recruit classmates to model your merch for you and then add in your branding digitally creating your own mock ups.

Your brand should include a (minimum) 10 page designed PDF that will walk the client through the following:
  • —Simplified Brand Strategy, Brand Values + Brand Idea
  • —A logo or logo system & signage if necessary
  • —Business cards
  • —Icons & illustrations
  • —A one page website, that is also designed for mobile
  • —Three merchandise ideas that are engaging (no logo slapping! ie. tote bag, mug or tshirt) For example: a plant store could have gardening gloves & seed packages, a bakery has napkins and an apron, a shaving company has razors and soap.
  • —Some art directed photos that add to substance to your brand


You will go through three rounds of revisions for your brand in the project, that will be looked at during a series of check ins.
