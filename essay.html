Designer Research Essay


Your aim will be to write a 750 word essay about a designer from the list below and then 'design' your essay in the style of the designer that you have chosen to research.

Your essay should fit in a 'booklet' of one 11 x 17 folded in half so you have 4 single pages & two double page spreads. Use color, typography and images to enlighten your audience about this designer’s work. You will print enough copies for the class to read the day that you present. Design in Indesign and make sure you spell check before you print.
