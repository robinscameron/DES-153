Utopia or Oblivion Infographic



In the book Utopia or Oblivion Buckminster Fuller proposed a list of questions that expanded idea that humanity has the opportunity to create a world where all human needs are met.

For this assignment you will explore the questions that he proposed in 1969 considering their relevance in 2022. After choosing a question create an infographic that attempts to enlighten humanity to the answer. Imagine that this infographic could belong in a Natural History or Science museum.

You will come up with your own data for the question you choose, and create an infographic that is easily understood. This infographic will be 11 x 17 poster size, horizontal or vertical, it should include your sources and present the information in an engaging way. Consider typography hierarchy, iconography, color schemes and appropriateness.
